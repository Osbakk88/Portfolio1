Hello there

My name is Christina, and I'm a Front-End Development student with a long background in healthcare. For many years I worked closely with people, routines and complex systems — and along the way I discovered how much I enjoy solving problems, creating order and making things easier for others.

That's what led me to front-end development. I love building clean interfaces, working visually in Figma and bringing designs to life with HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I'm curious by nature, detail-oriented, and I enjoy learning new tools — including using AI to improve workflows and code quality. I'm now working toward becoming a full-fledged front-end developer and building a portfolio that reflects both my creativity and my dedication.

My Journey

My journey into front-end development began with a growing curiosity for how digital solutions are built, and how good design can make information clearer and everyday tasks easier. Since starting my studies in January 2024, I've been steadily building my skills through hands-on projects, problem-solving and continuous learning.

What motivates me most is the process of turning ideas into something visual and functional — from early sketches in Figma to structured HTML, styled layouts and interactive JavaScript. Every project teaches me something new, whether it's accessibility, clean structure, responsiveness or better workflow.

As I progress through my studies, I'm focused on expanding both my technical skills and my ability to create intuitive, user-friendly experiences. This portfolio represents the beginning of that journey — and I'm excited for everything I'll continue to build and learn along the way.

AI & Development

One of my passions is exploring how AI can enhance the development process. I'm fascinated by AI-assisted coding tools and how they can help developers write better code, solve problems more efficiently, and learn new concepts faster.

I believe that combining human creativity with AI capabilities opens up exciting possibilities for building innovative web solutions.

Beyond Coding

In my free time, I enjoy reading books, watching movies, and spending time with my family and friends. I also love to travel and explore new places, which gives me fresh perspectives and inspiration for my work.

I am passionate about technology and how it can be used to improve our lives. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow, both personally and professionally.

Let's Connect

If you have any questions or would like to connect, feel free to reach out to me. I'm always open to discussing new projects, creative ideas, or opportunities to be part of your visions.
